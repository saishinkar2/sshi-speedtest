About SSHI SpeedTest

Our Mission

At SSHI SpeedTest, our mission is to provide users with a reliable and accurate tool to measure their internet speed. We believe that understanding your internet performance is crucial in today's digital age, whether you're streaming videos, working from home, or gaming online. Our goal is to empower users with the information they need to optimize their online experience.

How It Works

Our speed test works by measuring the speed of data transfer between your device and our servers. It assesses your download speed (how quickly you receive data), upload speed (how quickly you send data), and ping (the responsiveness of your connection). The test involves sending and receiving data packets and analyzing the time it takes for these packets to travel, providing you with a comprehensive overview of your internet connection's performance.
